BRIEF HISTORY

Holy Chapel is a megachurch and Christian denomination founded by Pastor Joe Mckeever in 1987. The Church, with the headquarters in Johnsonville,Monserrado Liberia, has since become a global network of churches, with congregations in many countries and has approximately 13,000,000 followers all over the world.
[1] The Church has ministries in Canada, Liberia, South Africa, the United States and the United Kingdom.
[2] The church has 145 branches, in five continents.[3]

OUR MISSION

Holy Chapel Missionary Baptist Church is a church whose primary mission is to introduce sinners to Jesus Christ through evangelism, to equip those who already know Him to know Him better and to serve Christ and His church effectively through discipleship, and to praise, honor, and glorify the living God: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. We are committed to understanding the evolving needs of the people, to providing excellent biblically based programs, to introducing Jesus Christ to the community, and to doing all things in an excellent manner, maximizing our resources to accomplish the greater good. More specifically, the mission is accomplished, and the purpose realized through the work of dedicated, devoted, and disciplined ministers, evangelists, and laypersons who work toward the advancement of the kingdom of God. Every teaching ministry is based on the saving work and Lordship of Jesus Christ, the centrality of His person, and the inerrancy of the Word of God. From this Life Source flows our evangelical perspective, our discipleship equipment, our ministry motivation, and our reason for being.
